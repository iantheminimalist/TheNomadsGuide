What is a Nomad

The Nomad’s Guide is a Traveling Hub for those who want to explore California’s Public Parks, Trails, Beaches, and Recreation services. To inform those who want to explore California’s wanderlust public destinations without breaking the bank. Users will be able to access a library of public spots, as well as contribute to the site by submitting their public findings, and share their experience through social media.
